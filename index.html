Our History

Belleville Dental was established in 2004 as a professional dental care practice that renders holistic, affordable, and quality dental services to meet the needs of our patients. Belleville Dental is a well-equipped clinic with modern dental facilities located at Akwa Ibom State. Belleville Dental deploys the latest technologies, with the capacity to provide world-class dental services in line with global best practices.

Our Vision

To be a world class dental clinic that renders standard and valuable dental healthcare services to all persons; irrespective of socioeconomic status thereby entrenching good oral health culture in our environment

Our Mission Statement

What To Expect

At Belleville Dental, you can expect to be treated like family. Your dental health is our top priority! We offer modern options and use the latest technologies in professional cleanings, oral exams, and digital x-rays.
